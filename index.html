Structural optimization · interactive

Give a computer a load, some supports, and a budget. It finds the shape.

That's topology optimization: instead of drawing a beam and checking if it holds, you hand the solver a design space and let it decide where material belongs. Both demos below run entirely on your device — real finite elements, no server, no install. Phones welcome.

STRUCTFORGE

Race the optimizer. Build a structure inside a fixed material budget while a live plane-stress FEA solve scores how stiff it is. Then let the SIMP optimizer spend the same budget and see how close you got.
